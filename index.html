Interesting Science Facts

  • Water can boil and freeze at the same time under special conditions called the 'triple point'.
  • Honey never spoils! Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3000 years old and still edible.
  • Bananas are berries, but strawberries are not!

Fun DIY Experiments

1. Invisible Ink with Lemon Juice

Write a message using lemon juice and heat the paper to reveal it.

2. Baking Soda Volcano

Mix baking soda and vinegar to create an erupting volcano effect.

3. Water Surface Tension Test

Sprinkle pepper on water and add a drop of soap to see the pepper scatter.
